To give a demonstration, a user could potential search using the following query:
"DR"

The result from before ranking the result, would then be a list of sites that contain words from the query (see \cref{fig:example_index}). The result would then simply be sorted on the "Score" key. The final top 3 result could then be something like:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item "https://dr.dk"
    \item "https://dr.dk/nyheder"
    \item "https://dr.dk/drtv"
\end{enumerate}
